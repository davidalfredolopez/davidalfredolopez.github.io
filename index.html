While a gained experience on my own printer, I saw that some of the printers on Unitec Labs were being unused do to malfuncitoning. We developed a prototype of computer vision based system using Python and OpenCV to count on the amount of clients that entered and came out of his business. Currently I'm strenghtening my Python knowledges and learning on HTML and CSS for web development.
