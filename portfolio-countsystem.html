Vehicle Counting System using Computer Vision

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Using Python, OpenCV, and YOLOv3 architecture with Darknet pretrained weights, we were able of prototyping an application capable of detecting objects like vehicles and people in video, classify them, and count if they were entering or exiting a local commerce. Currently ACCEPTED for publication and presentation in the LACCEI 2020 International Multi-Conference of Engineering, Education and Technology.
